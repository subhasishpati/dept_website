Mycomp Society

"MyComp Society, the pride of the Department of Computer Science Engineering and Applications, is a vibrant community that unites all students, faculties, and staff members from both the Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) and Master of Computer Applications (MCA) programs. Established in 2008, it has since thrived under the visionary leadership of Dr. Prof. Sarojananda Mishra, who serves as its esteemed President. Throughout the academic calendar, MyComp Society diligently organizes a plethora of technical and cultural events that enrich the academic and social fabric of the department. These events include hackathons that foster innovative thinking and problem-solving skills among students, celebrations of Teachers' Day to honor the dedicated educators, welcoming ceremonies for the freshers to integrate them into the MyComp family, and farewells to bid adieu to the graduating students, reminiscing on their journey within the society. Additionally, orientation programs are held annually to acquaint newcomers with the society's ethos and objectives. Beyond these events, MyComp Society serves as a platform for fostering creativity, collaboration, and camaraderie among its members. It stands as a testament to the department's commitment to holistic development, ensuring that students not only excel academically but also grow into well-rounded individuals prepared to face the challenges of the ever-evolving tech landscape."

Coding Club

"The Coding Club at the Computer Science Engineering & Application Department is a vibrant community that serves as a catalyst for innovation and collaboration among students passionate about coding and technology. Comprising enthusiastic learners and seasoned coders alike, the club fosters an environment where members can hone their programming skills, explore emerging technologies, and engage in creative problem-solving. Through workshops, hackathons, and coding competitions, students have ample opportunities to expand their knowledge and showcase their talents. The club also organizes guest lectures and industry interactions to provide valuable insights into the ever-evolving field of computer science. With a focus on fostering camaraderie and skill development, the Coding Club plays a pivotal role in shaping the future leaders and innovators of the technology industry."
