ICMIB(2023)

"3th International Conference on Machine Learning, IoT and Big Data (ICMIB-2023) aims to bring together leading academic scientists, researchers and research scholars to exchange and share their experiences and research results on all aspects of Machine Learning, IoT and Big Data. The Machine Learning, IoT and Big Data are characterized by the usage of smart solutions with embedded intelligence at the edge that relies on high connectivity, processing capabilities for edge devices and real-time analysis of information."

Technovation

"The Technovation event is a dynamic gathering that showcases cutting-edge advancements in technology, innovation, and digital transformation. Bringing together industry leaders, entrepreneurs, and tech enthusiasts, it offers a platform to explore emerging trends, discuss groundbreaking ideas, and foster meaningful connections. From keynote presentations to interactive workshops, the event provides valuable insights and networking opportunities aimed at driving innovation and shaping the future of technology."

Orientation

"A college orientation program is an introductory event designed to familiarize new students with the campus, academic resources, and campus life. Typically held before the start of the academic term, it includes informative sessions, campus tours, and social activities to help students transition smoothly into college life. The program often covers topics such as academic advising, campus facilities, student support services, and extracurricular opportunities, providing incoming students with essential information and a sense of belonging within the college community."

Abhyuthanum(Fresher)

"A "Fresher" event in an engineering college typically marks the welcoming of new students who have just enrolled in their first year of engineering studies. It's a celebratory occasion filled with various activities and programs aimed at integrating the newcomers into the college community. These events often include orientation sessions to familiarize students with campus facilities, academic resources, and college life. Additionally, there may be fun activities like icebreaker games, talent shows, or sports competitions to foster camaraderie among the incoming students. The event serves as an opportunity for new students to meet faculty, seniors, and peers, creating a supportive environment as they begin their academic journey in engineering."

Exodus(Farewell)

"Farewell is a bittersweet event where we bid adieu to cherished members of our community who are moving on to new adventures. It's a time to reflect on shared memories, celebrate achievements, and express gratitude for the contributions made. Though we'll miss their presence, we wish them the very best in their future endeavors and look forward to the paths they'll forge ahead."

Teacher's day

"Teacher's Day is a special occasion dedicated to honoring and appreciating the invaluable contributions of educators in shaping our lives and society. It's a time to express gratitude, respect, and admiration for the dedication, guidance, and wisdom imparted by teachers. From inspiring curiosity to fostering growth, teachers play a pivotal role in nurturing future generations and igniting the flames of knowledge. On this day, let's celebrate the profound impact of teachers and express heartfelt thanks for their unwavering commitment to education."
